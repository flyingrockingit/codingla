Educational Board

The school is registered under the IGSCE Edexcel and Cambridge International boards.

Junior and Senior School

Junior School: Our Junior School caters to students from Kindergarten to Grade 6. It focuses on building a strong foundation in academics, creativity, and social skills. The environment encourages curiosity and active learning.

Senior School: Our Senior School provides education for Grades 7 to 12, preparing students for international examinations and future academic pursuits. With a focus on critical thinking, leadership, and specialized streams, students are equipped to excel in their chosen paths.
