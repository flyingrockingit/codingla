MY FAVORITE

Kadhi

Kadhi or karhi is a yogurt-based dish originating from Rajasthan, India which is made by simmering yogurt with besan (gram flour) and spices until it forms a thick, tangy gravy.

Dosa & Idili

Dosa and idli are both popular South Indian dishes made from fermented rice and lentil batter, but differ in their shape and cooking method. Dosa is a thin, crepe-like pancake that is pan-fried, while idli is a fluffy, steamed cake.

Payesh

Payesh is very popular Bengali dish of Kheer. Payas in Sanskrit means milk. This is a creamy rice pudding delicately flavored with cardamom and filled with nuts like badam, pistachios etc giving an amazing color with inclusion of saffron strands.

Sev Puri

Sev puri is an Indian snack and a type of chaat. It is a speciality that originates from Mumbai, Maharashtra, India.
