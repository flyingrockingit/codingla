About Flowers

Flowers are one of the most beautiful and diverse creations of nature. They come in various shapes, colors, and sizes, and they often symbolize love, beauty, and growth.

Fun Facts About Flowers

  • Roses are related to apples, raspberries, cherries, and almonds!
  • Sunflowers can grow up to 12 feet tall in just six months.
  • Some flowers bloom only at night, like the Moonflower.
  • The world's largest flower, the Rafflesia arnoldii, can weigh up to 15 pounds!
